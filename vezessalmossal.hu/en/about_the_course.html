The first 30-60 minutes of the course is a level test in which I determine your current skill level. The following sessions will be tailored to you accordingly.If there is a specific area that you would like to improve, we will focus on that, but the ultimate goal is to prepare you for every possible situation. This involves various bigger and smaller fields, some of which are listed below.

Basic fields:

  • proper usage of gear stick and pedals
  • precise steering, directional control
  • smooth braking and stopping
  • maneuvers, parking
  • driving in the city
  • intersections, right of way

... and more.

Advanced fields:

  • safe overtaking
  • driving on the highway
  • determining proper vehicle position
  • advanced braking, emergency braking
  • flow of traffic, driving dynamics
  • driving etiquette

... and even more.
